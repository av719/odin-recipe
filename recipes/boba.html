Bubble Tea

Description

Homemade bubble tea is an easy drink to make. It's my favorite type of drink!

Ingredients List

  • Tapioca Balls
  • Desired Flavor Powder
  • Water
  • Honey
  • Milk or Half-and-Half
  • Ice

Instructions

  1. Cook tapioca balls and mix with honey
  2. Make tea
  3. Add ice and tea to cocktail shaker
  4. Assemble
  5. Pour in milk
