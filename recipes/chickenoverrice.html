Chicken Over Rice

Description

Homemade chicken over rice is an easy dish to make. It's my favorite type of food!

Ingredients List

  • Rice
  • Chicken Thigh
  • White Sauce
  • Hot Sauce
  • Salad
  • Pita Bread

Instructions

  1. Cook rice
  2. Cook chicken
  3. Make white sauce
  4. Make hot sauce
  5. Assemble with salad and pita bread
